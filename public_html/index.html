Introduction

As a die-hard football fan, have you ever watched football, and say to yourself, “Geez, I could coach a team better than that!”? Well, now you can! Fantasyfootball4u.com is absolutely FREE fantasy football web site that allows you to create your own team, just the way you desire! You can get together with friends and/or family to create your own league and have a friendly competition to see who can create the best team! You can pick your own players, and create a new starting lineup (with those players) each week to compete within your own league. Each week scores will be accumulated as your players do well, so choose your players wisely. Create your own account with us and let’s let the fun begin!
